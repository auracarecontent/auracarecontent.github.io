Plan to Action ‚Äî Digestive Posture Reset (5 minutes)

You're not fixing digestion today.
You're giving your gut room to breathe.
Step 1 ‚Äî Sit With Space (1 min)
Sit upright but soft.
Let your ribs stack gently over your pelvis.
No slouching. No stiffness.
Step 2 ‚Äî Belly Release (1 min)
Place one hand on your belly.
Let it soften ‚Äî no pulling in.
Step 3 ‚Äî Abdominal Breathing (2 min)
Inhale through your nose, allowing your abdomen (not your chest) to gently expand.
Exhale slowly, letting the abdomen soften and fall back.
Think: gentle expansion, not effort.
Step 4 ‚Äî After-Meal Cue (1 min)
After your next meal, check posture and breath for just one minute.
