Why It Matters

Both heat and movement support detox pathways, but they send different signals.
Choosing the wrong one for your current state can increase fatigue.
Choosing the right one can restore clarity and ease.
Understanding this helps prevent burnout and supports consistency.
It also reduces the urge to follow rigid rules.

What's Really Happening

The key player here is the autonomic nervous system.
Heat exposure tends to activate relaxation responses when the body is ready.
Gentle movement increases circulation and muscle pumping.
When energy is low, heat may calm without demanding effort.
When energy is stable, movement helps lymph and blood return to the heart.
Forcing either under exhaustion activates stress responses.
This is why matching support to energy level matters.
