How Posture Triggers Head Pain

Over time:
  • pain signals travel upward

Plan to Action ‚Äî Headache-Specific Reset (5 minutes)

Today, you're not fixing posture.
You're releasing built-up pressure that has already reached the head.
Step 1 ‚Äî Eye Reset (1 minute)
Most posture-related headaches start with overworked eyes.

Close your eyes
Place your palms gently over your eye sockets (no pressure)
Breathe slowly

This helps calm the muscles at the back of the head and temples.
Step 2 ‚Äî Neck Drain (1‚Äì2 minutes)
This is not a stretch.

Tilt your head slightly to one side
Make very small, slow circles ‚Äî about the size of a coin
Imagine tension draining downward, not being pulled
Switch sides.

The goal is release and drainage, not lengthening.
Step 3 ‚Äî Shoulder Weight Drop (1 minute)
Headaches often mean your shoulders are still carrying load.

Lift your shoulders toward your ears
Hold for 2 seconds
Drop them sharply down on the exhale
Repeat 3 times.

This contrast teaches the nervous system how to let go.
Step 4 ‚Äî Head Lightness Cue (1 minute)
Sit comfortably.
Imagine:

shoulders becoming heavy
the neck softening
the head feeling lighter than before

Don't straighten or correct posture.
Just let the head stop being a weight.
