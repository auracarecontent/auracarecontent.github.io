How energy comes back

Stress, irregular rhythm, and nutrient gaps all reduce the cell's ability to create ATP — the molecule that powers every action.
Here's what quietly drains your spark:
🔥
Oxidative load from stress, alcohol, or processed foods creates free radicals that tire cells.
🩸
Low oxygen delivery (low hemoglobin or ferritin) limits fuel for ATP production.
🥗
Low nutrient diversity weakens antioxidant defenses.
🌙
Disrupted circadian rhythm (late nights, screens) impairs nightly repair cycles.
Together these small imbalances lower your energy baseline and make recovery slower.
Cell Metab 2023; Front Physiol 2022; Clin Nutr 2023.
