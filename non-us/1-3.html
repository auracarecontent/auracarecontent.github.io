Why Focus Fades

Skipping meals, high-sugar snacks, dehydration, and chronic stress all destabilize your brain's fuel system.
When glucose drops or rises sharply, neurons fire less efficiently.
The prefrontal cortex ‚Äî your decision-making center ‚Äî "dims," reducing focus, memory, and motivation.

Meet Your Brain's Fuel System

Your brain thrives on a steady energy supply:
