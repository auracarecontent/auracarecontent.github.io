Why It Matters

Inflammation often builds from small daily stress signals. So it lowers the same way.
Not from one extreme reset. But from predictable habits that tell your body it is safe.
Safety allows repair. Repair lowers internal irritation.
üß†

What's Really Happening

One important organ in daily inflammation control is the liver.
The liver filters your blood. It breaks down used hormones and waste products. It then packages part of that waste into bile.
Bile carries waste into the gut so it can leave the body.
When hydration, sleep, and regular meals are steady, bile flows more consistently. Waste exits smoothly.
When habits are chaotic, bile flow slows. This can feel like heaviness, sluggish digestion, and low clarity.
