What's Really Happening

The key player today is your heart rate pattern.
During relaxed walking, your heart beats faster than at rest, but without sharp jumps.
This creates a smooth cycle of filling and pumping inside the heart chambers.
Blood moves forward evenly instead of in bursts.
That steady circulation reduces internal strain and keeps oxygen delivery consistent.
The nervous system reads this pattern as safe and manageable.
In daily life, this often feels like calm energy ‚Äî awake, but not rushed or edgy.
