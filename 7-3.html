How Gut Inflammation Affects the Brain

Your gut lining acts as a selective barrier: nutrients pass through, harmful particles stay out.
When this barrier becomes irritated or overly permeable:
  • immune cells activate,
  • inflammatory signals enter circulation,
  • the brain receives a message: "Stay alert."
This process is often called leaky gut ‚Äî not as a diagnosis, but as a functional state.
The brain responds to inflammation by:
  • lowering cognitive flexibility,
  • increasing sensitivity to stress,
  • reducing motivation and focus.
It's not weakness. It's protection.
