Meet the Hidden Calm Switch

The vagus nerve isn't only deep inside your body ‚Äî parts of it reach your skin through small sensory branches around your ears, neck, and jaw.

Why It Matters

Most people carry hidden tension in the neck and jaw. Constant phone posture, long sitting, or shallow breathing keep muscles tight and the vagus under-active.
